The authors utilize Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA), a generative probabilistic model for topic modeling, to classify literature related to children's speech disorders. The method involves processing text corpora from academic publications and identifying latent topics that characterize the research landscape. The LDA model assumes documents are mixtures of topics and topics are mixtures of words, enabling automatic categorization without predefined labels.
