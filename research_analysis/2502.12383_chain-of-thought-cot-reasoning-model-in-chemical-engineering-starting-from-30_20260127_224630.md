This research addresses the fundamental challenge of applying generative Artificial Intelligence to "hard science" domains, specifically within Chemical Engineering and Heavy Oil processing. As experimental data becomes increasingly high-dimensional and complex, standard Large Language Models (LLMs) often fail to provide the rigorous, transparent reasoning required for scientific analysis. Unlike general-purpose tasks, chemical engineering demands that AI conclusions align with established physicochemical principles. The authors identify a critical gap where opaque probabilistic models lack the specialized logic necessary to derive valid insights from raw experimental outputs, risking data misinterpretation in critical industrial applications.

By forcing the model to explicitly display its inference path, the "Deployed Chain" mimics the iterative deduction process used by human experts.
