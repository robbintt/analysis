## Executive Summary

> **Critical Insight:** Traditional Knowledge Graphs (KGs) treat domains as implicit background, leading to rigid, context-independent definitions that bottleneck advanced AI.

This research introduces the **Domain-Contextualized Concept Graph (CDC)** framework, solving the "context problem" by elevating domains to first-class structural elements. Replacing static triples with a **C-D-C format** (`<Concept, Relation@Domain, Concept'>`), the framework utilizes an `@` operator for granular, nested domain scoping (e.g., `HighSchool@Math@Calculus`).

Implemented natively in **Prolog**, the system transforms relations into executable predicates capable of automated logical inference.
