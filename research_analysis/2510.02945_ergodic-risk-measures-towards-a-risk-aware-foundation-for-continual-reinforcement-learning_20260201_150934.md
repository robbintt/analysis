## Key Findings

*   **Current Limitation:** Continual RL research is predominantly limited to risk-neutral decision-making, focusing solely on optimizing expected long-run performance.
*   **Theoretical Incompatibility:** Classical risk measure theory is theoretically incompatible with the demands of continual learning.
*   **New Metric Class:** A novel class of measures, termed **'ergodic risk measures,'** establishes compatibility between risk-aware decision-making and continual learning.
*   **Empirical Advantage:** Evidence demonstrates that ergodic risk measures offer intuitive advantages in balancing the retention of useful information with adaptation to new situations.
