## Executive Summary

## Key Findings

*   **The Uncanny Valley of Agency:** User comfort decreases significantly when interacting with AI that is highly agentic but behaves erratically.
*   **Cognitive Breaches and Dissonance:** AI failures are perceived not merely as errors, but as cognitive breaches caused by a lack of understanding of the system's logic.
*   **The Quasi-Creature Paradox:** User frustration stems from the mental conflict between the AI's superhuman linguistic fluency and its lack of physical embodiment or consistent agency.
*   **Efficiency-Frustration Correlation:** The study identified a strong negative correlation between perceived AI efficiency and user frustration levels.
*   **Ontological Root:** User experience issues with generative AI are identified as ontological problems (related to the nature of being) rather than purely technical glitches.
