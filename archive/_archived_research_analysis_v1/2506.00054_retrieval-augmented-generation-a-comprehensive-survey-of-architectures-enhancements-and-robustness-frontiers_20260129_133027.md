## âš™ï¸ Technical Details

### Definition & Framework
RAG is defined as a framework coupling pretrained LLMs with non-parametric retrieval modules. The generation probability is modeled as a conditional distribution approximated via a Top-k retrieval mechanism:

> $P(y|x) \approx P(y|x, d_i) \approx P(d_i|x)$

### Pipeline Stages
1.  Query Processing
2.  Retrieval (Sparse: BM25; Dense: DPR; Hybrid; Generative)
3.  Re-ranking
4.  Generation
5.  Post-processing

### Architecture Categories
*   Retriever-Centric
*   Generator-Centric
*   Hybrid
*   Robustness-Oriented

### Specific Patterns & Techniques
*   **Query-Driven Retrieval:** RQ-RAG, GMR, RAG-Fusion, KRAGEN, LQR.
*   **Retriever-side Adaptation:** Re2G, SimRAG.
