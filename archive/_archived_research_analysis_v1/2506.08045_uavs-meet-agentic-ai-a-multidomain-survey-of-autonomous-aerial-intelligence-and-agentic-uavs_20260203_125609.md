## üìù Executive Summary

This paper addresses the fundamental limitations of traditional Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs), which are restricted to SAE Level 1-2 autonomy and rely on static waypoint navigation and single-modality sensing. As UAV applications expand into complex, unstructured environments, there is a critical need for systems capable of goal-driven behavior and high-level decision-making.

Surveying seven high-impact domains‚Äîincluding precision agriculture, disaster response, and logistics‚Äîthe authors highlight that current systems lack the necessary flexibility for dynamic real-world scenarios. Furthermore, the study identifies significant barriers to widespread adoption, including technical constraints, regulatory limitations, and critical issues regarding data-model reliability.

The core innovation is a comprehensive conceptual framework for **"Agentic UAVs"** based on the Vision-Language-Action (VLA) paradigm. The survey provides a comparative analysis demonstrating that Agentic UAVs significantly outperform traditional systems by utilizing learned RL policies, multimodal sensing, and energy-aware path optimization. The findings point toward the development of self-evolving aerial ecosystems, emphasizing the necessity of sustainable deployment and equitable governance.

## ‚öôÔ∏è Technical Details

The paper proposes a **four-layer hierarchical architecture** for Agentic UAVs following the Vision-Language-Action (VLA) paradigm.

### System Architecture Layers

1.  **Perception Layer**
    *   **Function:** Multimodal sensor fusion.
    *   **Inputs:** RGB, thermal, and LiDAR data.
    *   **Processing:** Utilizes CNNs and Transformers.

2.  **Cognition Layer**
    *   **Function:** Decision-making and policy generation.
    *   **Techniques:** Reinforcement Learning (RL) and probabilistic modeling.

3.  **Planning and Control Layer**
    *   **Function:** Trajectory generation, swarm coordination, and reflective control.

4.  **Communication Layer**
    *   **Function:** Connectivity and interaction.
    *   **Protocols:** V2X (Vehicle-to-Everything) and Natural Language processing.

### Enabling Technologies

*   **Edge AI Hardware:** Platforms such as NVIDIA Jetson are essential for low-latency processing.
*   **Vision-Language Models (VLMs):** Models like Flamingo and LLaVA are utilized for instruction following and semantic understanding.
