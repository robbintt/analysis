# Swivuriso: The South African Next Voices Multilingual Speech Dataset

## Quick Facts
- **arXiv ID:** 2512.02201
- **Source URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.02201
- **Reference count:** 0
- **Primary result:** 3000-hour multilingual speech dataset for seven South African languages with improved ASR performance across agriculture, healthcare, and general domains

## Executive Summary
Swivuriso presents a comprehensive 3000-hour multilingual speech dataset covering seven South African languages across agriculture, healthcare, and general domains. The dataset includes both scripted and unscripted speech collected through community-centered, ethically grounded practices. It addresses significant gaps in existing automatic speech recognition (ASR) resources by providing broader domain coverage and deeper speaker diversity than previous datasets. Baseline experiments demonstrate improved ASR performance using models like Whisper, Wav2Vec-BERT, and MMS, with observed reductions in word error rates across multiple languages.

## Method Summary
The dataset was developed through community-centered data collection practices emphasizing ethical considerations and cultural sensitivity. Speech data was gathered across three domains - agriculture, healthcare, and general conversation - in seven South African languages. Both scripted prompts and unscripted natural speech were recorded to capture diverse linguistic patterns. The collection process prioritized speaker diversity and included measures to ensure ethical handling of participant data. Multiple recording environments were utilized to capture varied acoustic conditions representative of real-world usage scenarios.

## Key Results
- 3000-hour multilingual speech dataset covering seven South African languages
- Performance improvements across baseline models (Whisper, Wav2Vec-BERT, MMS) with reduced word error rates
- Comprehensive coverage across agriculture, healthcare, and general domains with both scripted and unscripted speech
- Enhanced speaker diversity compared to existing South African language ASR resources

## Why This Works (Mechanism)
The success of Swivuriso stems from its comprehensive approach to addressing the data scarcity problem for African languages. By collecting 3000 hours of speech across multiple domains and both scripted and unscripted formats, the dataset captures the linguistic diversity needed for robust ASR models. The community-centered collection methodology ensures authentic speech patterns and cultural relevance, while the emphasis on speaker diversity helps models generalize across different accents and speaking styles. The multi-domain coverage enables models to handle domain-specific terminology and contexts, particularly important for specialized areas like agriculture and healthcare where precise terminology is crucial.
