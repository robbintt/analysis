# ArzEn-MultiGenre: An aligned parallel dataset of Egyptian Arabic song lyrics, novels, and subtitles, with English translations

## Quick Facts
- arXiv ID: 2508.01411
- Source URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.01411
- Reference count: 17
- Manually aligned parallel dataset with 25,557 segment pairs of Egyptian Arabic and English

## Executive Summary
ArzEn-MultiGenre presents a high-quality parallel corpus containing Egyptian Arabic song lyrics, novels, and subtitles aligned with English translations. The dataset was constructed using professional translators and language experts to ensure translation quality and alignment accuracy. It addresses a gap in existing resources by covering genres previously underrepresented in Egyptian Arabic-English parallel corpora, making it valuable for machine translation benchmarking, LLM fine-tuning, and translation studies research.

## Method Summary
The dataset was created through manual translation and alignment processes involving professional translators and language experts. The corpus includes three distinct genres: song lyrics, novels, and subtitles, each requiring different translation approaches. The alignment process ensured that source and target segments were properly matched at the segment level, creating a high-quality parallel corpus suitable for various NLP applications.

## Key Results
- Contains 25,557 segment pairs of Egyptian Arabic and English
- Covers three previously underrepresented genres in Egyptian Arabic corpora: song lyrics, novels, and subtitles
- Suitable for machine translation benchmarking, LLM fine-tuning, and translation studies research
- Has pedagogical value for training translation students and serving as translation memory for professionals

## Why This Works (Mechanism)
The dataset's effectiveness stems from professional-level translation quality and careful manual alignment, ensuring semantic equivalence between Egyptian Arabic and English segments. The inclusion of diverse genres captures different linguistic registers and translation challenges, from the poetic nature of song lyrics to the narrative complexity of novels and the time-constrained nature of subtitles. This diversity makes the corpus valuable for developing robust translation systems that can handle various Egyptian Arabic text types.

## Limitations
- Limited to Egyptian Arabic and English, excluding other Arabic dialects
- Manual alignment process may introduce inconsistencies or biases
- Dataset size may be insufficient for training large-scale models without augmentation
- Focus on specific genres means limited coverage of other Egyptian Arabic usage domains
