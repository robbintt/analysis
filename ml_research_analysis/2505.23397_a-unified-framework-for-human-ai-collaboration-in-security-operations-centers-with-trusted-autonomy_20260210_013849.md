## Executive Summary
This paper presents a theoretical framework for human-AI collaboration in Security Operations Centers (SOCs) with an emphasis on "trusted autonomy." The framework aims to address the challenges of alert fatigue and skill shortages in SOCs by creating a structured approach to AI-assisted security operations. However, the paper lacks empirical evidence, implementation details, or validation studies to support its claims. The proposed framework remains conceptual without documented real-world testing or measurable outcomes.
