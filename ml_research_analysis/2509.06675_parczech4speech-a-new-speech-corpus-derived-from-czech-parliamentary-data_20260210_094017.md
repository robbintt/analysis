# ParCzech4Speech: A New Speech Corpus Derived from Czech Parliamentary Data

## Quick Facts
- **arXiv ID:** 2509.06675
- **Source URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.06675
- **Reference count:** 30
- **Primary result:** 2,695 hours of aligned Czech parliamentary speech from 587 speakers

## Executive Summary
ParCzech4Speech 1.0 is a new Czech speech corpus derived from parliamentary recordings, containing up to 2,695 hours of aligned speech from 587 speakers. The dataset addresses the lack of large, flexible, and commercially accessible Czech speech resources by providing three variants: sentence-segmented for ASR/TTS tasks, unsegmented for continuous speech scenarios, and raw alignment for custom refinement. Using WhisperX and Wav2Vec 2.0 for automated recognition and alignment, the pipeline extracts more data with higher reliability than previous versions. The corpus includes rich metadata and is released under a CC-BY license, making it suitable for both academic and commercial applications. It provides 27.8M aligned words with 25.2M achieving perfect alignment, and supports various speech modeling tasks.

## Method Summary
The corpus was created by processing Czech parliamentary recordings from 2012-2022 using automated speech recognition and alignment techniques. The pipeline employed WhisperX for enhanced alignment and Wav2Vec 2.0 for acoustic modeling, processing approximately 4,000 hours of raw audio data. Three dataset variants were produced: segmented versions for ASR and TTS tasks, continuous recordings for scenarios requiring uninterrupted speech, and raw alignment data allowing users to refine the automatic segmentation. The processing achieved significant improvements over previous versions by extracting more data with higher reliability through the combination of these advanced speech processing tools.

## Key Results
- 2,695 hours of aligned speech from 587 speakers
- 27.8M total aligned words with 25.2M achieving perfect alignment
- Three dataset variants: sentence-segmented, unsegmented, and raw alignment
- Released under CC-BY license for commercial and academic use

## Why This Works (Mechanism)
The success of ParCzech4Speech stems from leveraging parliamentary recordings which provide high-quality, diverse, and legally accessible speech data. The combination of WhisperX for precise alignment and Wav2Vec 2.0 for robust acoustic modeling enables efficient processing of large volumes of audio while maintaining accuracy. The three-variant approach addresses different use cases: segmented data for precise ASR/TTS tasks, continuous recordings for natural speech scenarios, and raw alignment for customization needs.

## Architecture Onboarding

**Component map:** Raw parliamentary recordings -> Preprocessing -> WhisperX alignment -> Wav2Vec 2.0 features -> Three variant outputs

**Critical path:** Audio processing pipeline: Recording ingestion → Noise reduction → WhisperX alignment → Wav2Vec 2.0 feature extraction → Dataset variant generation

**Design tradeoffs:** The choice of automated processing over manual annotation enabled large-scale corpus creation but may introduce systematic alignment errors. The parliamentary domain provides legal accessibility but may not fully represent all Czech speech variations.

**Failure signatures:** Poor alignment quality in noisy segments, underrepresentation of non-parliamentary speech patterns, potential speaker overlap with existing datasets.
