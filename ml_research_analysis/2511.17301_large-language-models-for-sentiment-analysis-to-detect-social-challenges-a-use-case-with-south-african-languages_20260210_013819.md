## Executive Summary
This paper explores the potential of large language models (LLMs) for sentiment analysis focused on detecting social challenges in South African languages. The study proposes using LLMs as a tool for analyzing sentiment in multilingual contexts, with a specific focus on South African linguistic diversity. The research aims to address the gap in sentiment analysis capabilities for underrepresented languages in Africa, potentially enabling better understanding of social issues through language analysis.
